Who We Are & What We Do

Registered legally as Medical Innovations & Supplies Uganda Misu Limited (MISU Limited), we operate at the core of Uganda's healthcare and biomedical logistics ecosystem. As an essential middle-tier bridging agent, we source international, CE-certified medical technology from top-tier global manufacturers and efficiently channel them directly into the domestic health grid.

Our Strategic Value Chain

We specialize in business-to-business (B2B) supply metrics and corporate health procurement frameworks. By managing rigid regulatory clearance standards and product safety setups, we ensure local facilities stay stocked with resilient clinical resources.
